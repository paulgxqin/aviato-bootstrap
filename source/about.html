About Resyo

Guangxi Liyang Garments Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive sportswear manufacturing enterprise that integrates in-house research and development, production, sales, and services. Since its establishment in 2007, the company has continuously won a good market reputation for its comfortable style, personalized design, and exquisite craftsmanship.

The factory boasts a fully equipped production base with a workshop area of approximately 5,000 square meters, equipped with over two hundred imported and domestic garment-making machines. This enables the independent production of various kinds of knitted and woven sportswear and various professional sports apparel products.

The factory primarily produces fitness and running series of quick-drying shirts, shorts and long pants, T-shirts, polo shirts, jerseys, sports jackets, and other garments, with a daily output of over 8,000 pieces. It has a large-scale spot warehouse supply chain, and its products are sold to multiple regions at home and abroad, serving multiple e-commerce platforms and physical wholesale supply, supporting drop shipping, and offering personalized services such as processing and customization according to samples or designs.

Over the years, Liyang Garments has achieved steady improvement in product quality and sales channels through scientific, in-depth, and refined production management. With accurate market positioning, a design style that is both classic and fashionable, yet caters to the mass market, and a humanized management philosophy, we sincerely invite friends from domestic and foreign businesses to visit our factory for inspection and guidance, to discuss cooperation, and to create a win-win situation!
